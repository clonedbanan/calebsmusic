Google Sites embeds the Netlify page in one iframe, while Spotify is embedded in a second iframe. Browsers do not reliably allow a custom button in the outer page to start media in the nested Spotify iframe.

In this version:
- On the standalone Netlify site, the album button controls the Spotify embed.
- Inside Google Sites, the album button opens the exact Spotify song in a new tab or the Spotify app.
- The official Spotify embed below remains playable directly inside Google Sites.

This is a browser/iframe security limitation, not a Netlify deployment problem.
